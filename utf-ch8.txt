The <meta charset= "UTF-8"> tag in HTML servers an essential purpose: 
It stands for Unicode Transformation Format 8-bit
It defines the character encoding for the document. 
Character encoding determines how charactes are represented in binary form within a document.
It is widely used character encoding that supoorts a vast range of characters from different languages and symbols worldwide.
By including this mata tag in HTML documents, you ensure that browsers interpret the content correctly.
UTF-8 covers almost all the characters and symbol and it is suitable for multilingual websites. 
UTF-8 ensuers proper display and interpretation of the text, regardless of the language or special charactes used.
